How to contact Cash Support

To speak to a Cash Support representative, please contact support through your Cash App, cash.app/help or our phone line.

How do I contact Cash Support through the App?

  1. Tap the profile icon on your Cash App home screen
  2. Select Support
  3. Select Start a Chat and send a message

    Or Navigate to the issue and tap Contact Support

How do I call Cash Support on the phone?

You can reach Cash Support by calling 1 (800) 969-1940. We're here to help every day from 9 AM to 7 PM ET.
